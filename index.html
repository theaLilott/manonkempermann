Manon Kempermann

I am a forth-semester bachelor’s student studying Data Science and AI at Saarland University, Germany. Alongside my studies, I work as a research assistant at the Interdisciplinary Institute for Societal Computing. My current research focuses on understanding the marginal risk of AI. In another project I try to understand the impact of fine-tuning on safe-guards of LLMs.

I am passionate about AI safety, both in the technical and governace aspect, and believe that it is one of the most pressing issues at this time. In my blog here, I want to share my journey into the field and try to break concepts, problems, the whole technicality down to be understandable for everyone.
